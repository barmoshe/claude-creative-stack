## 1. TL;DR

V1 is run editorially by **Netanel ("Neta") Livneh**, formerly editor-in-chief of *Yedioth Ahronoth*, who in 2026 was promoted to chief editor over Keshet Digital's mako, V1 and 12+ properties while continuing to lead V1 [(ice.co.il "שינויים בקשת דיגיטל" — search snippet)](https://www.ice.co.il/tv/news/article/1110900) [(walla brnz — search snippet)](https://b.walla.co.il/item/3833244). The Keshet Digital unit that hosts V1 is led by CEO **Uri Rozen** with **Lior Yaniv** as VP Content; the V1 newsroom is described in the Hebrew Wikipedia entry as "about 20 video editors and content staff" reporting up through Yaniv [(he.wikipedia "V1" via search snippet)](https://he.wikipedia.org/wiki/V1) [(zoominfo Lior Yaniv profile — snippet)](https://www.zoominfo.com/p/Lior-Yaniv/2593237803). Above Keshet Digital, **Avi Nir** has been Keshet Media Group CEO since 2002 and was reaffirmed in Variety's 2025 list [(Variety500 Avi Nir)](https://variety.com/exec/avi-nir/) [(Keshet International press)](https://www.keshetinternational.com/ki-news/variety-honors-avi-nir-ceo-of-keshet-media-group-for-8th-consecutive-year). The single most-cited 2026 fact: V1's editorial leadership is now expanding its remit across all of Keshet Digital — i.e. V1 has just become a *senior* lever inside Keshet, not a side project [(ice.co.il — search snippet)](https://www.ice.co.il/tv/news/article/1110900).
