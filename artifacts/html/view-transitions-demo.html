View Transitions — same-document morph

Click any swatch. The grid item morphs into a detail view via document.startViewTransition(). Click "Back" or anywhere outside to return. Baseline since Oct 2025; falls back to instant DOM swap on older browsers.

Heads up: your browser does not support the startViewTransition API. The demo still works — it just swaps state instantly.
