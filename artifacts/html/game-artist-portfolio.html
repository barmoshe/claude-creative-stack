An introduction, printed in haste.

I paint the quiet
rooms of games.

Six years of painting skylines that you walk past without noticing, and then, a week later, miss. I'm most useful in the bit of production where the mood has not been decided yet and someone has to make a decision about the color of the sky. I like fog that explains things. I like silhouettes that do all the work before the detail pass starts. I've shipped four titles, led small teams on two of them, and I still keep a paper sketchbook because the computer is too fast.
