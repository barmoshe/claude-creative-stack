# Cold-open patterns

The cold open is the most important real estate on the channel. 5–10 seconds, sells or kills.

## Pattern 1 — Sound first

> 0:00 — 4 bars of the demo loop.
> 0:05 — Title card; audio ducks 6 dB.
> 0:08 — VO: "I built this in a weekend."

Best when the demo is the surprise. Use whenever the audio carries the meaning unaided.

## Pattern 2 — Question + bait

> 0:00 — VO: "What if you could sequence drums by drawing circles?"
> 0:03 — A weird visual answers instantly (a spinning circle, drum hits firing on its dots).
> 0:08 — Title card.

Best when the build's premise is the surprise.

## Pattern 3 — Bug-as-feature

> 0:00 — A glitchy, half-broken beat plays.
> 0:03 — VO: "This was supposed to be a drum machine. It became a glitch instrument."
> 0:08 — Title card.

Best when the bug is the most-listenable thing in the take.

## Pattern 4 — Before/after

> 0:00 — Standard 4-on-the-floor kick. VO: "Here's what most drum machines do."
> 0:03 — Cut to the build's output: a polyrhythmic pattern.
> 0:06 — VO: "Here's what mine does."
> 0:08 — Title card.

Best for builds where the contrast tells the story.

## Pattern 5 — On-camera reaction

> 0:00 — Demo loop plays. Camera holds on the creator's face.
> 0:04 — Reaction (laugh, headshake).
> 0:06 — VO: "Okay so I need to explain how this happened."
> 0:08 — Title card.

Best when the creator is on-camera and the reaction is real.

## Forbidden

- A logo intro before any sound.
- The word "today" (filler).
- "What's up everybody" or any greeting before the hook.
- Long IDE shots before the demo plays.
- Any cold open where AI/Claude is mentioned before the music.
