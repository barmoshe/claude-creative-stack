# Audience

## Who they are

- Bedroom producers and music tech-curious developers.
- Comfortable in a DAW (Ableton, Logic, Reaper, FL).
- Skeptical of "AI" framings; respect tools that work.
- Watch on phones (assume mobile-first thumbnails).

## What they want

- A new toy they can try in the browser by the end of the video.
- A "huh, that's a clever way to use Claude" moment.
- The musical wow at the end. They came for the sound.

## What they hate

- Clickbait that doesn't deliver.
- Long intros before any sound plays.
- Code crowding the screen with no demo.
- "I'm using AI to build…" without showing the actual workflow.
- Generic AI-art thumbnails.

## Where they hang out

- r/edmproduction, r/synthesizers, r/audioengineering, r/ableton.
- Producer Discords.
- Hacker News (occasionally — when a tool is novel).
- Twitter/Bluesky synth + audio-dev communities.

## What "good comments" look like

- "Can you add MIDI export?"
- "What happens if you crank the swing past 100%?"
- "Tried this on my phone, the latency is fine."
