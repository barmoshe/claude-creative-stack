# Channel Identity

## Promise

> "I build real music tools with AI as my dev partner — then prove them with sound."

## Three doctrinal lines (do not deviate)

1. **Promise.** Above.
2. **Headline rule.** Sell the musical result FIRST. The AI workflow is supporting evidence, not the headline.
3. **Closing rule.** Every episode ends with sound, not code.

## Voice

- Curious, not hyped.
- Honest about what didn't work.
- Plain English over jargon.
- "Here's the bug, here's the fix" beats "watch me code flawlessly".

## Values

- Real tools, not demos that only work on stage.
- Browser-first builds — viewers can try the live app.
- Bugs are a beat, not a hidden cost.

## What this channel is not

- Not "watch AI write code". The viewer is here for the music.
- Not generation-API tutorials (Suno, Udio, Lyria). Tools, not tracks.
- Not a code-review channel.
